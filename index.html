This is the Pumpkin pie!

  • 1 can pumpkin puree (15 oz)
  • 1 can evaporated milk (12 oz)
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp cloves (optional)
  • 1 pie crust

This is the apple pie!

  • 6-8 cups sliced apples (like Granny Smith)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp flour or cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp butter (to dot on top)
  • 2 pie crusts (bottom + top)
  • 1 egg + 1 tbsp milk (for crust wash)

This is the cherry pie!

  • 4 cups pitted cherries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter (on top)
  • 2 pie crusts (bottom + top)
  • 1 egg + 1 tbsp milk (for crust wash)
